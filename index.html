Artist Statement

As an artist, I create artworks to convey my feelings for the subject matter. Many of the subjects are characters or objects from my favorite videogames and Japanese animation.

Creating art allows me to interact with many artists in person and through social media. I love how my art work can bring many different types of people together and allow us to interact together without discrimination. When I view my artworks, I see myself slowly growing, becoming more confident in my art style and as a person. What motivates me to continue creating artwork is knowing that I made someoneâ€™s day when they view my artwork. My goals are to explore new techniques, form new bonds, create new memories, and inspire many artists to draw what they love.
