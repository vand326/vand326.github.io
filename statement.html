Artist Statement

My name is Vanna Dao, I am a student attending San Jose State University majoring under Digital Media Art. As an artist, I care about conquering my fears as an artist, improving my artwork, and allowing others to see my art. In my work, I enjoy creating something that has my personality, interest, and life. For this, I have created four projects.: my friend’s birthday adventure, Food Color flow projection, childhood memories with the internet.

In my art, the things I’ve included are people and things that are important to me and how some things represent me and I want to show that’s who I am. My friends are very important to me and sharing how our adventure was or how our hangout are is something I’ve always wanted to do like a small vlog. The food color flow represents how my life is, how everyday I go with the flow and experience all kinds of events and emotions. My childhood always watching animation and enjoying seeing colors and it felt like a dream that I wish I can go back to.
