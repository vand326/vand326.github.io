"Detective Saihara", Digital Art, Clip Studio Paint & Photoshop, 2018

I drew this character from one of my favorite games, New Danganronpa V3. I used wacom intuos tablet to sketch in Clip Studio Paint. I created a new layer and drew an outline. Then I colored using the oil brush tool. When I finished drawing my image, I brought it to Photoshop and used the hue/saturation tool to enhance the colors and the quality of the image.
