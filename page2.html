"Black Hamburger", Digital Art, 2018


I created a unique color hamburger that can be found in Japan's Burger King. In this project, I tried many new things I've never done before. I've never drawn food before and decided to draw with colors we don't usually see. I drew the sketch on clip studio paint and painted over on that same sketch layer. Later, I brought it to Photoshop to enhance the colors and the quality of the image.
