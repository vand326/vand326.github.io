"Love Room", Maya, 2019


I want to express how love is like. How love can be hypnotizing. There are two human figures with different patterns on them. The checker table is to represent how love is a puzzle and a challenge. There is a heart in the center to show that it's the main goal. I used Maya to create these 3D models. I used the polyshape and deformed it by changing the vertex position and extruding the faces to create the body.
